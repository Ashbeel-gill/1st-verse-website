FAQ

Fair questions, straight answers.

What does “$15/hour, all-in” actually include?

Everything. The agent’s time, recruiting, three weeks of brand training, ongoing QA scoring, team management, and the tooling they work in. No onboarding fee, no platform fee, no minimum. You’re billed to the minute, and the Monday ledger itemizes every one of them.

Are these really humans? No AI anywhere?

Every reply your customers receive is written by a named human agent. We use software for routing and QA analytics behind the scenes — never for customer-facing conversation. It’s in the contract: no bots, ever.

How fast can we actually go live?

The connection takes about 60 seconds — helpdesk, store, Slack. Your agents then train on your past tickets and macros for one to three days, and go live in a shared Slack channel where you watch every reply from day one.

What if the quality isn’t there?

Every conversation is QA-graded against your rubric. If an agent scores below 95, we retrain them on our own time, not your invoice. And the first 7 days are free — if we’re not obviously better than what you have, walk away owing nothing.

Can you cover nights, weekends and spikes?

Yes — follow-the-sun teams in Lisbon, Manila and Bogotá cover 24/7. For launches and holiday spikes we pre-stage extra trained agents on your account so response times hold while volume triples.
